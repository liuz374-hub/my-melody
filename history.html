My Melody Timeline

1975 — The Beginning

My Melody was first created by Sanrio designer Yuku Tanaka. She was originally designed as a character to appeal to children who loved cute rabbit characters.

1976 — First Merchandise

The first My Melody merchandise was released in Japan, including stationery items and small plush toys that became instant hits among children.

1980s — Growing Popularity

My Melody's popularity grew significantly during this decade. The character became a staple in Sanrio's character lineup and began appearing on various products worldwide.

1990s — International Recognition

My Melody gained international recognition and became one of Sanrio's most beloved characters. The character was featured in animated series and comics.

2000s — Modern Evolution

My Melody evolved with the times while maintaining her classic charm. New product lines were introduced, and the character remained popular with multiple generations of fans.

2010s — Digital Age

My Melody expanded into digital platforms with mobile apps, social media presence, and collaborations with modern brands and designers.

2020s — Continuing Legacy

My Melody continues to inspire joy and friendship. The character remains a beloved icon of kindness and creativity, celebrated by fans around the world.
